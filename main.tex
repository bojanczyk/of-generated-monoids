\title{Which automata are monoids?}
\author{Miko{\l}aj Boja\'nczyk, Bartek Klin, and Jingjie Yang}

\maketitle 
\begin{abstract}
    In orbit-finite sets, deterministic automata are strictly stronger than monoids. We give an algorithm which decides the difference, i.e.~it inputs a deterministic orbit-finite automaton, and decides whether it is equivalent to an orbit-finite monoid. 
\end{abstract}
